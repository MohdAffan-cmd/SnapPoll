## ğŸ¯ Usage

### Creating a Poll

1. Click **"Create a Poll"** on the home page
2. Enter your question
3. Add poll options (minimum 2)
4. Select duration (1 hour, 12 hours, or 24 hours)
5. Optionally hide results until expiry
6. Click **"Create Poll"**

### Voting on a Poll

1. Open the poll link
2. Select your preferred option
3. Click **"Vote"**
4. View real-time results (if not hidden)

### Sharing a Poll

1. Navigate to the poll page
2. Click the copy button next to the poll URL
3. Share the link with others

## ğŸ¨ Features in Detail

### Time-Limited Polls
Polls automatically expire after the selected duration. Once expired, voting is disabled and final results are displayed.

### Real-Time Updates
Vote counts and percentages update in real-time as votes are cast. Visual progress bars show the distribution of votes.

### Anonymous System
No user accounts required. All polls and votes are anonymous, ensuring privacy and ease of use.
